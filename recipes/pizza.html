Pizza Dough

Description

This pizza dough makes a soft and fluffy crust on the inside with a slightly crispy outside. It is easy to prepare and perfect for homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • ½ teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix warm water, yeast, and brown sugar in a bowl and let sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Add olive oil and salt to the mixture.
  4. Mix in part of the flour until combined.
  5. Knead the dough and gradually add remaining flour.
  6. Place dough in an oiled bowl.
  7. Cover and let it rise for about 1 hour.
  8. Punch down the dough and shape it.
  9. Bake with toppings at 220°C until golden.
