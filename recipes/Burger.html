Juicy Lucy Burger

Description

This burger is a famous burger stuffed with melted cheese inside the patty. It is juicy, flavorful, and served in a bun with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic salt
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, and garlic salt in a bowl.
  3. Form the mixture into 8 thin patties.
  4. Cut each cheese slice into smaller pieces.
  5. Place cheese between two patties and seal the edges tightly.
  6. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  7. Cook patties for about 4 minutes on one side.
  8. Flip and cook the other side until fully cooked.
  9. Serve on buns and enjoy.
